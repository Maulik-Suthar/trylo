Company Profile

Trylo Intimates is one of the most well-known brands in lingerie segment of India. The reason behind achieving this mark is Trylo Intimates' innovative and comfortable solution. Trylo Intimates has carved out a niche for itself and has become the first choice of millions of women.

The journey of Trylo Intimates began in 1992 and today it has reached to the mark of 10,000+ retailers across India. Rather than focusing on fashion, Trylo Intimates comes out with innovative products which help overcome the problems faced by the target audience.

Trylo Intimates is identified for its quality products and stands true on its proposition of 'Inner Luxury'. Trylo Intimates has also addressed the plus size niche in an effective manner.
